How This Works

Every claim is cryptographically verifiable:

  • Identity: Ed25519 signatures prove who issued each receipt
  • Content: SHA-256 hashes bind input and output data
  • Provenance: Parent links form tamper-evident DAG
  • Inclusion: Merkle proofs show receipt is in the log
  • Trust: Stake computed algorithmically from public receipts

No platform. No central authority. Just cryptographic math.
